About Upper13

A simple little helper that uses statistical learning to predict the half of the wheel in which the ball will land in. Train it by feeding it visual ballistic data and it will help you to consistently beat the game. Keep in mind that your roulette AI is only as good as the data you feed it - good eye -hand coordination required! Furthermore, the app is build for real physical roulette games and will not work on online roulette games or digital roulette simulations. The app is only meant for fun games and should not be used in real money gambling. All prediction made by the app are highly dependent on the ability of the user and are stated as probabilistic recommendations rather than deterministic outcomes.
